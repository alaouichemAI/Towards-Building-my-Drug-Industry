About us

At the intersection of chemistry and technology, Chemisters is a newly launched initiative focused on practical, reproducible tools that bring ML and automation to everyday lab operations.

We believe that every chemical discovery should be backed by robust data, validated protocols, and reproducible methodologies. Our team combines deep chemistry expertise with cutting-edge AI and automation technologies.

Based in Rabat, Morocco, we serve clients worldwide, delivering innovative solutions that bridge the gap between traditional chemistry and modern technological capabilities. Our approach emphasizes practical implementation over theoretical promises.
